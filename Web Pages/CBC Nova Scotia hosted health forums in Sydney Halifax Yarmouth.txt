We hosted public forums in three different communities during the month of February. The Search: A look at Nova Scotia's physician shortage Sydney CBC Nova Scotia was in Sydney on Feb. 7 at the Joan Harriss Cruise Pavilion. We asked: Does every Nova Scotian really need a family doctor? WATCH HERE Halifax We hosted a forum in Halifax on Feb. 13.  We wanted to know: Are politicians hurting health care?
